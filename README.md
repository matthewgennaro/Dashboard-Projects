Projects:

1. Customer Order and Product Fulliment of Fudgemart and Fudgeflix

Using Power BI, Snowflake, dbtCloud, and SQL, I developed two comprehensive dashboard suites using FudgeFlix and FudgeMart datasets. The customer dashboard tracked detailed order histories with KPIs and allowed filtering by month, department, product type, and company through interactive visual elements. The product fulfillment dashboard monitored product sales goals versus actual performance and enabled filtering by year, division, department, and product category. Both projects focused heavily on stakeholder usability, drill-down capabilities, and eliminating manual reporting through automated measures and structured data models.

2. Professional Sports Interaction With Local Crime

   In one Tableau project, myself and 3 others analyzed the relationship between local professional sports team performance and crime trends across major U.S. cities. I designed an interactive dashboard that enabled side-by-side city comparisons, incorporating team performance metrics alongside categorized crime data. The dashboard allowed users to dynamically explore trends, filter by city, and evaluate correlations in a clear, executive-ready format. This project strengthened my ability to align data modeling, UX design, and storytelling into a cohesive reporting product.
